About

I am an IoT and Robotics Engineering student with hands-on experience designing embedded systems that solve practical problems in healthcare, environmental monitoring, and industrial automation. My engineering approach emphasizes reliability, scalability, and measurable outcomes.

My work spans the complete development cycle: circuit design and PCB prototyping with ESP32 microcontrollers, sensor integration and data acquisition, embedded firmware development, machine learning model deployment on edge devices, and cloud-based data visualization using MQTT-based architectures.

I have published research at IEEE conferences and built production-grade systems tested in real-world environments. I am actively seeking remote engineering roles and international research or scholarship opportunities where I can contribute to impactful technical challenges.
